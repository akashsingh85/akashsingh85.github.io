Guidelines for Developers

Developers should follow ethical standards to create software that prioritizes user privacy and security. Ethical practices help ensure technology benefits everyone.

Ethical Considerations for Data Scientists

Data scientists must avoid biases in algorithms and ensure transparency in data collection and use. Ethical decision-making builds trust with users and organizations.

Privacy and Security

Protecting user data is essential in the IT field. Professionals must comply with regulations to safeguard personal information from misuse.
